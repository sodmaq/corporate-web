Our History

From Lagos to the World Stage

Nexvora Group was founded in 2009 by Adaeze Nwosu and James Osei in a small office on Victoria Island, Lagos. Armed with combined experience from McKinsey and Goldman Sachs, they set out to build a firm that understood the African business context as deeply as any global strategy house—but moved faster and partnered more closely with clients.

By 2013, we had expanded into East Africa and South Africa, advising governments and multinationals navigating the complexities of rapid economic development. Our first international office opened in Nairobi, followed by London in 2016 and Dubai in 2019.

Today, Nexvora Group operates across 18 countries with over 420 professionals and has delivered transformative outcomes for more than 340 clients—from sovereign wealth funds and global banks to technology startups reshaping entire sectors.

What We Stand For

Core Values

01

Intellectual Honesty

We tell clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. Our value comes from being right, not from being agreeable. We challenge assumptions, test hypotheses, and present findings with complete transparency.

02

Outcome Ownership

We measure ourselves by client results, not deliverable quality. Our engagements are structured around agreed outcomes, and we remain accountable—through implementation, adjustment, and proof of impact.

03

Contextual Intelligence

Global frameworks applied without local knowledge fail. We invest deeply in understanding the specific economic, regulatory, cultural, and competitive context of every market we work in.

04

Collaborative Excellence

The best thinking emerges from diverse perspectives working in concert. We build inclusive teams—internally and with clients—that combine complementary strengths to tackle complex problems.

05

Long-Term Thinking

We optimize for 10-year outcomes, not 10-quarter ones. Our advice accounts for sustainability, resilience, and the compounding effects of strategic choices made today on organizational health tomorrow.

06

Talent as Catalyst

Exceptional people drive exceptional outcomes. We invest relentlessly in developing our team and help clients build the internal capabilities to sustain and accelerate progress beyond our engagement.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

2009

Founded in Lagos

Adaeze Nwosu and James Osei establish Nexvora Group with 8 consultants and a focus on financial services strategy in West Africa.

2012

First Major Government Contract

Selected to lead a national financial inclusion strategy for the Central Bank of Nigeria, reaching 12 million unbanked citizens.

2015

East Africa Expansion

Opened Nairobi and Johannesburg offices, expanding our team to 85 professionals and doubling revenue within 18 months.

2018

London & Dubai Offices

Established European and Middle East presence to serve multinational clients expanding into African and emerging markets.

2021

Digital Practice Launch

Launched our dedicated Digital Transformation practice, combining technology implementation with strategic advisory for accelerating digital-first growth.

2024

$2.4B Value Milestone

Cumulative client value created surpasses $2.4 billion. Team grows to 420+ professionals across 18 countries on 4 continents.
