Frequently Asked Questions

Working With Us

We work with a broad range of organizations—from multinational corporations and private equity-backed businesses to government agencies and development finance institutions. Our clients typically share a common characteristic: they're facing a consequential strategic challenge or transformation that requires deep expertise and committed partnership. While we serve organizations across many sectors, we have particularly deep expertise in financial services, energy, telecommunications, and public sector.

Each engagement is scoped individually based on your specific challenge and objectives. We typically begin with a diagnostic phase (2–4 weeks) to define the problem and align on scope, followed by an analytical phase, a design phase, and a delivery/implementation phase. Engagements range from focused 6-week strategy sprints to multi-year transformation programs. We agree on clear milestones, deliverables, and success metrics before work begins.

Our fees vary based on scope, complexity, team composition, and duration. Short diagnostic projects typically start from $80,000 USD, while large-scale transformation programs can range significantly higher. We price based on value delivered, not just time spent, and we're happy to discuss flexible arrangements for the right opportunities. Contact us for a preliminary scoping conversation—there's no obligation.

We operate a senior-led model. The partner who leads your proposal will lead your engagement—not hand it off after signing. Every team includes at least one senior consultant or manager with direct relevant experience, and partners remain actively involved throughout, not just at key milestones. We'll introduce you to the full engagement team before work begins so you can review and approve the composition.

Project timelines vary considerably. A focused strategy project might run 6–12 weeks. A market entry program could span 4–8 months. A full digital transformation or post-merger integration program may run 12–36 months. We help you think clearly about what can be accomplished in what timeframe, and we'll be honest when ambitious timelines are unrealistic.

Our Approach

Three key differences: first, we have deeper on-the-ground expertise in African and emerging markets—not imported frameworks applied remotely, but genuine contextual knowledge. Second, we stay through implementation. Many global firms deliver recommendations and exit; we embed, execute, and are accountable for results. Third, you get senior attention throughout, not a team of junior analysts supervised remotely by a partner you rarely see.

Careers

Our process typically runs 3–4 weeks and consists of: (1) CV and cover letter review, (2) a 30-minute introductory call with HR, (3) a case study interview with a senior consultant, (4) a written case exercise, and (5) a final-round interview with a partner. We aim to give candidates a realistic preview of the work and make offers promptly to strong candidates.
